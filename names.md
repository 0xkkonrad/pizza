# Name proposals

Konrad & Natalia pick one. Domain availability checked 2026-07-05 via RDAP: ✅ = unregistered at check time, ❌ = taken, (?) = not checked yet. Caveat: ✅ means *registrable*, not necessarily standard price — dictionary-word and 2-letter `.pizza` names are often registry-premium; verify the actual price at Porkbun/Cloudflare before falling in love.

Collision sweep (verified — details in [research.md](research.md) §4): **hard avoid** Pizza Diary/Diaries (thepizzadiaries.com is an active exact-name review blog); **soft avoid** Pizza Journal (Scott Wiener's turf) and Cornicione (pizzerias worldwide). Verified clean everywhere: **Crown & Char** (cleanest of all), **Alveolation**, **Slice Notes**; **We Ate Pizza** nearly clean (weeatpizza.com placeholder + "We, The Pizza" DC chain nearby). Re-check the Instagram handle right before buying.

## Shortlist (my picks)

1. **We Ate Pizza** — `weatepizza.com` ✅ — memoir is past tense. Earnest, funny, zero pretension; says *couple* and *diary* in three words, with a pleasant "We Rate Dogs" echo. Exact name verified free (neighbors: weeatpizza.com placeholder, "We, The Pizza" DC chain).
2. **Crown & Char** — `crownandchar.com` ✅ — lifted from our own review vocabulary, alliterative, looks beautiful in a minimal masthead. Verified the cleanest option checked: nothing named this anywhere, in any field.
3. **Alveolation** — `alveolation.com` ✅ — one word, deeply nerdy, verified free everywhere. Reads like a zine title. Trade-off: subculture vocabulary, so harder to search-own than a coined phrase.
4. **Beauty: Normal** — `beautynormal.com` ✅ — deadpan, straight from the template's funniest line. Very memoir.
5. **normal.pizza** (as name *and* URL) ✅ — the wildcard: minimal, absurd, unforgettable.
