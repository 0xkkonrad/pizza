We are Konrad and Natalia, and we eat a lot of pizza. Not professionally — we have jobs — but attentively, and usually while disagreeing.

This is where we write it down. Not often: a good pizza is worth the trip and the wait, and neither of those happens every week. When one does, it gets an entry here.

Every entry opens with the same nine lines — Style, Base, Crown, Cheese, Tomatoes, Olive oil, Leoparding & char flavour, Alveolation, and Beauty. We borrowed the vocabulary from people far more serious than us, and we keep the order fixed so that a pizza in Naples and a pizza on a Tuesday at home can be held up against each other years apart. After the list comes the part we actually care about: where we were, who we were with, and what we said on the way home.

There are no scores. There is never a number out of ten. "Beauty: normal" is about as damning as it gets, and even that we'll take back if the pizza was kind to us. If you want a leaderboard, the internet has several excellent ones. This is just the two of us, remembering dinner.
