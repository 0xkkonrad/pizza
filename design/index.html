My read

You asked for very minimalistic + memoir + lightweight, and the research says your edge is the structured list + a diary voice with no score. Ranking against exactly that:

  1. The Paper (1) — my pick. It is the name, carries the memoir voice best, and stays feather-light (system serif, zero JS). Minimal in the "quiet and considered" sense, not the "bare" sense.
  2. Tasting Card (3) — the boldest bet. It makes your one genuinely novel move — the attribute list — the whole identity. Pick this if you want to look unlike anything else in pizza.
  3. Bear Bones (2) — the safe floor. If in doubt, start here; it's five minutes of setup and impossible to get wrong. Easy to graduate to The Paper later.

Field Notes (4) and Trattoria (5) are the two "more" options — more photos, more character — there in case minimal isn't actually what you want once you see it.

A strong hybrid worth a word: The Paper's masthead + typography with Tasting Card's boxed spec block for the nine fields — literary frame, structured heart. Say the word and I'll mock that up as #6. Detail & sources: research.md.
